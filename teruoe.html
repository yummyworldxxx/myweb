Yeoh——!! The okonomiyaki I had yesterday, 26/1/3, at Nanjamonja (Nagoya City, near Shiogamaguchi Station) was insanely good. It’s been a full day and I’m still moved by it. One of the reasons I created Yummy is that *how* and *what* you eat is absolutely crucial to how good something tastes. Without that, there’s no reproducibility, and the emotion doesn’t come across. You can’t share the same level of “this is amazing.” And even at the same restaurant, what you order changes everything. Like how the soy-sauce ramen can be incredible, but the salt version is just okay. In this case, adding spicy sauce and mayonnaise to the deluxe okonomiyaki brings out every single element of what makes okonomiyaki delicious. Honestly, part of me thinks it could’ve been a 5.0. Haha. It was really that good. Man, it was amazing. That feeling—pure emotion.
